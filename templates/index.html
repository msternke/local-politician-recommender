About this project

What can I do with this app?

Using this app, you can compare local politicians who you may want to learn more about to more renowned politicians that you recognize.

Why may this be helpful?

Voter turnout in local political elections can be incredibly low. While this is a a very nuanced problem, one major contributing factor is that voters are generally unfamiliar with local political candidates. This app will help voters learn more about their local politicians, and hopefully inspire more engagement in local politics by doing so.

How can we compare the politicians?

Comparing politicians across different levels of government can be difficult; they do not vote on common legislation. However, these days nearly all politicians share one thing in common: they Tweet! Nearly all politicians use Twitter as a political platform to directly connect with voters and broadcast their political ideologies. Thus, a politician's collection of Tweets can represent an effective means to compare politicians.

How can we compare politicians' Twitter profiles?

To analyze the politicians, I scraped the most recent Tweets for a number of politicians. Using these most recent Tweets, I apply various techniques of natural language processing to:
1. Identify the most important words Tweeted by the politicians
2. Determine the sentiment of Tweets containing these words
This analysis gives me thousands of word vectors by which to compare the politicians. Similarities between politicians are then quantified using cosine similarity as a similarity metric.
